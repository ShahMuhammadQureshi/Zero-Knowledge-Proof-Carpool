Registration

Introduction:

Welcome to the Registration Page of our carpooling service! To access the full features of our platform and enjoy the benefits of carpooling, you need to create an account. Registration is quick, easy, and free!

Instructions:

To register, please fill out the form below with your personal information, including your first name, last name, email address, and password. You'll also need to select your role as either a passenger or a driver. Once you've completed the form, click the "Register" button to create your account. After registration, you'll be able to log in and start using our carpooling service right away.
